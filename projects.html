Projects

Phase 1 – Smart Management of Hydroponics Using IoT

Developed a system using a color sensor and water level sensor connected to an ESP8266 for IoT monitoring.

Integrated a water pump that activates automatically based on sensor readings, ensuring efficient water management.

Focused on real‑time sensing, wireless control, and automation as the foundation for later energy harvesting integration.

Phase 2 – Smart Management of Hydroponics Using IoT with Piezoelectric Energy

Designed a system using piezo discs connected in parallel to capture energy from footsteps.

Energy was stored in a supercapacitor and regulated through a TP4056 module to safely charge a Li‑ion battery.

Integrated with Blynk IoT for real‑time monitoring of voltage and battery status, with outputs powering small devices like sensors and displays.
